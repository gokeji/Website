More about me

Are you better today than you were yesterday?

I am not perfect, yet I am a perfectionist. Very often I find myself trapped in a search for perfection, be it a perfect solution to a problem, a perfect attire for an occasion, or a perfect way of life. However fruitless these attempts may seem at first sight, I do believe I owe a great deal of my knowledge to this "personality flaw".

I am not perfect, but I keep growing and evolving. I ask myself every day, "Are you better today than you were yesterday?" If I can say yes, I am one step closer to the perfection I seek.

Be not angry that you cannot make others as you wish them to be, since you cannot make yourself as your wish to be.

I consider myself an empathetic person, perhaps even someone who values others over myself. While that means I did have to put in some extra effort in realizing the "self", I am happy to be making constant efforts to step into other people's shoes.

This makes me a great person to work with. While I am still critical, because of the high standards I uphold myself, I try to understand where others are coming from and convey my ideas in a way they can accept. That said, I still most enjoy working with people who also make an effort to understand me.

Learn, to discover how ignorant you are, then learn to appreciate that.

There was a time when I felt wiser whenever I learned something new. Now, that feeling is often coupled with the acknowledgement of how lacking I really am. It puts me in shame to ever assume to be wise, and pushes me to learn more. When I look back, though, it delights me to think that younger Gao would be very proud of me.

I love learning new skills. I sing, play guitar, cook Chinese food, work out, swim, play table tennis, and write songs. But above all, I love learning new languages, because they open me up to new perspectives in life. Besides English, Chinese, and Japanese, programming languages and music are among those languages that I cherish to be learning.
